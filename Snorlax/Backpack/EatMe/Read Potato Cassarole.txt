1 lbs thick-cut bacon, cooked and chopped
2 lbs red potatoes, washed and diced
2 lbs chicken breast, cooked and chopped
1 cup sour cream
8 oz cream cheese
8 oz shredded mild cheddar cheese
8 oz shredded Colby jack cheese
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 scallion, chopped
 

1) Preheat the oven to 400Â°F

2) Melt cream cheese over a double boiler. Mix in sour cream, cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined.

3) Add potatoes, chicken and 3/4 of chopped bacon, mix well

4) Pour the concoction into a greased 9x13 baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 60-75 minutes, until potatoes are fork-tender. 

5) Remove from the oven. Stir. Sprinkle Colby Jack cheese over top and bake for 15 more minutes uncovered.

6) Remove from oven and sprinkle casserole with what's left of the bacon and the scallions.

7) Enjoy!

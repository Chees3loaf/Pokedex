1 3/4 cups flour
1 tablespoon baking powder 
2 eggs 
1 3/4 cups milk 
1/2 cup cooking oil or shortening, melted 
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tbsp vanilla extract

1) Mix ingredients thoroughly in a large mixing bowl until consistent.

2) Pour 1/3 cup more or less into the grid of a preheated, greased waffle iron.

3) Top with 1 pad of salted butter and syrup to balance flavors

4) Enjoy!

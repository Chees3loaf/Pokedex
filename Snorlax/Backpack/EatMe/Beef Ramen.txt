1 pound ground beef 85/15
2 tablespoons garlic, powdered or minced
1 tablespoon ginger, powdered or minced 
1/4 cup Sake or white wine
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup mirin
1/2 cup light soy sauce
2 packets ramen discard the seasoning packet
1 sweet onion
1 bell pepper
sesame seeds

1) Brown the ground beef in a heavy bottomed skillet on medium-high heat until cooked through (roughly 6-8 minutes). Season as desired.

2) Add in the garlic and ginger, cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.

3) Add in the white wine, sugar, mirin and soy sauce.

4) Let reduce on medium heat for 6-8 minutes or until reduced by half the volume. (Add a cup of water if it reduced too much)

5) While the sauce is reducing steam your onion and bell pepper slightly in the microwave for 1-2 minutes in a covered microwave safe bowl.

6) Add onions and bell pepper to skillet and toss together while sauce is reducing.

7) Cook the ramen for 1 minute shy of the instructions, strain and add to the skillet. 

8) Toss together before serving. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired. 

9) Enjoy!

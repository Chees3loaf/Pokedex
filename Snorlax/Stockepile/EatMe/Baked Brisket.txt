1 fresh beef brisket (4 to 5 pounds)
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon browning sauce, (kitchen bouquet or Maggi Jugo seasoning sauce)
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons pepper
1 tablespoon Los Calientes hot sauce or your favorite hot sauce (red works best)
Dash of Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning

1. Place brisket, fat side down, in a 13x9-in. baking dish. In a small bowl, mix Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, onion powder, browning sauce, salt, pepper and hot sauce; pour over brisket. Turn brisket fat side up; dust with some Tony Chachere's and refrigerate it overnight covered.

2. Remove brisket from refrigerator and let it sit for 15min. Preheat oven to 300Â° and bake covered for 4 hours.

3. Remove brisket from oven and let the meat rest for 15min before uncovering. Cut diagonally across the grain into thin slices.

4. Enjoy!
